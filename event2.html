Hackathon Competition

Welcome to our annual Hackathon Competition! This is an exciting opportunity for participants to showcase their coding and problem-solving skills.

Event Details:

  • Date: November 10
  • Time: 10:00 am to 12:00 pm
  • Location: CCF Lab

Competition Format:

Participants will form teams and work collaboratively to solve challenging problems within a specified time frame. The competition will cover various domains, including but not limited to web development, mobile app development, and data science.

Prizes:

Exciting prizes await the winning teams, including cash rewards, sponsor swag, and the opportunity to showcase their projects to industry professionals.

Registration:

Registration is open to individuals and teams. Please register by 9th November to secure your spot in the competition. Don't miss this chance to be part of an innovative and engaging event!
