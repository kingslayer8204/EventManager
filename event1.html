Poster Presentation

The poster presentation competition is an opportunity for students to showcase their research and creative projects in a visual format. The competition is open to all undergraduate and postgraduate students from any discipline. The posters will be judged by a panel of experts based on the following criteria:

  • Originality and significance of the research or project
  • Clarity and coherence of the poster design and layout
  • Quality and accuracy of the content and references
  • Presentation skills and ability to answer questions

The competition will be held on November 10, 2023 from 10:00 am to 12:00 pm at the Auditorium. The winners will be announced at the end of the session and will receive attractive prizes and certificates.
